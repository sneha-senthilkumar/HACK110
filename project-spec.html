Bark-Meow-Mooooo!

Welcome! We invite you to partake in a reimagined tic-tac-toe!

Animal lovers will truly adore this spin on traditional X's and O's of tic-tac-toe!

Click here to play.

Player 1 will be a cute kitten and player 2 will be a little puppy!

Jump scare alert! The victor gets to experience a wild, though domesticated, surprise!!!

Designers: Amy Clark, Alana Fiordalisi, Sneha Senthilkumar

COMP110 Programming Concepts:

  • Functions
  • For Loops
  • Arrays
  • Classes, Constructors, and Methods
  • If-Then Statements
  • Mouse Events
  • HTML
  • Anonymous Functions

Challenges included multiple failures in getting the background and characters to appear properly.

New concepts we learned include HTML sound elements, clearing the screen, and general game development skills.

Most of this project was collaboration, some individual innovations included:

  • Sneha: HTML, array of quadrants
  • Alana: Modulo (for player order), halted duplication of background
  • Amy: Sound elements, enhanced alignment of characters
